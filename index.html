About our hotel

A best place to enjoy your life

We as Safari Hotel, offer you a blend of Victorian elegance and comfort, combined with all the practical, executive amenities of the modern age. Antique furnishings and restored period details envelop guests in royal mystique. Our exceptional suites symbolise all the comfort, warmth and elegance of the “Safari Hotel style” par excellence. The prestigious suites pay homage to their famous past residents. The fine settings and ambience offered by the our hotel suites, allows you to have a soothing and enchanting stay at one of the most premium hotels in Westlands,Kenya.
